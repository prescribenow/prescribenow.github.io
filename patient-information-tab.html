Patient Information Tab

Before describing patient information in detail, it is important to explain the ‘Encounter tiles’ located at the top of this tab. All Encounters arranged against a patient’s name are tiled here for the provider to see. A provider may click on ‘Resume’ to carry forth a patient encounter from the point it was discontinued. To understand what an ‘Encounter’ is, please refer to @Encounters.

If you click on ‘View All’ at the upper right corner, it will take you to Encounters Tab. Refer to @Encounters Tab. The patient information tab contains basic information about a patient, including date of birth, age, biological sex, address, phone, etc.

Patient Information can be edited by selecting the ‘Edit’ link. A new window opens titled ‘Edit Patient’. Input all changes that are required. Refer to @Patient Onboarding for details of these fields. Click on ‘Update’ to confirm and save the modifications made.

‘Substance Use’ includes substance dependence and recent substance use. Substance usage can also influence treatment decisions, medication choices, and the management of withdrawal symptoms.
‘Chronic Conditions’ are long-lasting health conditions that persist over an extended period, typically for years or a lifetime.

‘Insurance Eligibility Check’ is a feature that verifies and provides information about a patient's insurance coverage.
‘Billing History’ refers to a record of financial transactions related to patient charges, payments, and invoicing.

It plays a crucial role in ensuring accurate billing and financial transparency. BH records all payments that were raised against a patient’s name and the amount received. It also displays outstanding balance (if any) and the method of payment (not shown in the screenshot).

Payments are the financial transactions related to settling bills or invoices for healthcare services provided. Payments are accepted in three ways; cash, credit card or check. Every payment method has its dedicated screen and corresponding text fields.

Selecting 'Submit Payment' clears the remaining balance and enables you to generate the receipt.

Kindly note that if there are no pending payments associated with a patient's name, no information will be visible under the payments section.
