Patient Portal

A patient’s portal setup is different than a Provider’s portal. The interface is simpler and contains fewer options.

At the left part of the screen are four options, namely: Home, Appointments, Prescriptions and Messages.
‘Home’ is used to bring user to the main screen, regardless of what screen is open. ‘Appointments’ displays all appointments booked against the patient. Prescriptions reveal particulars of medication that the patient has been using for some time. Messages show messages that the patient has exchanged with healthcare providers regarding his or her disease.
