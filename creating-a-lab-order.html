Lab Order

A lab order is a request from a physician to a clinical laboratory to perform certain tests on a patient.

Creating a Lab Order

1. To create a lab order, select ‘Patients’ from the home screen.

2. Select the patient's name to be referred for a lab test.

3. Click on ‘New’ at the far right of your screen and select ‘Lab Order’. You will arrive at the following screen:

4. Clicking on ‘Next’ button or Selecting ‘Specimen Information’ opens a list of patient specimens that you may refer to a lab. The ‘Other’ option contains a text field that you may use to enter specimen information which is not listed. If you select ‘Fasting’, please enter the number of hours your patient should be fasting before giving a lab sample.

5. Select ‘Next’ to confirm your specimen choices. This will open ‘Labs Requested’.

6. Enter specific test(s) name in the text field you want to perform on patient and click on ‘Add’.

8. Enter any ‘Additional Tests’ that you may require and give reasons why the test should be prescribed in the text field labelled ‘Reason for Test’ for better understanding of lab attendant.

9. Search for ICD-10 codes that adhere to your prescribed tests. For example, for urine tests, select from one of the test codes shown here.

10. The ‘Summary’ outlines all the test options, and details, that you have selected. After you confirm that all ordered tests are necessary and in the interest of the patient, you may print the Lab Test Request.

11. Below is a sample Lab Test Request.

12. An overview of lab order details is also displayed on the main screen of the Patients tab. You can edit or download the lab order from here as well.

There are many other ways to initiate a new Lab Order:

  • Encounters tab view > Assessment and Plan > Add Lab Order (bottom right corner)

  • SOAP Note > Scroll down to Plan section > Add Lab Order

  • Patients > Specific Patient’s name > New (top right corner) > Provider Note > Add Lab Order
