Appointments

If the ‘Appointments’ page is selected, it will display all appointed dates and time, provider’s name, chief complaint and diagnosis of the patient.

At the right side of the panel, another ‘Appointment’ heading is displayed. Recent patient’s appointments are displayed here with two navigation buttons. Clicking on the navigation buttons (right and left) allows a patient to peruse all of his or her appointments and their details. If ‘View All’ (adjacent to the navigation buttons) is clicked, a screen same as above is displayed.

Three symbols are located immediately to the right of the 'Diagnosis' entries. The first symbol is used to display the ‘Patient Visit Summary’. One sample is shown here. Selecting ‘Export as PDF’ will download the summary to your device.

The second symbol is used to download the above-mentioned summary.
The third symbol is for printing the summary.
