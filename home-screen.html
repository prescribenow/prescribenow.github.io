Home Screen

The ‘Home’ screen typically provides a central hub where users can access essential features and information including appointment schedules, prescription updates, and relevant notifications.

Recent patient’s appointments are displayed under ‘Appointment’ heading with two navigation buttons. Clicking on the navigation buttons (right and left) allows a patient to peruse all of his or her appointments and their details. If ‘View All’ (adjacent to the navigation buttons) is clicked, you will redirect to @Appointments page.

If any payment is to be made for an appointment, the ‘Make Payment’ button may be clicked. An invoice with provider’s name, patient’s visit details and amount to be charged are displayed. Patient is required to fill in his or her credit card details and billing address particulars. Clicking on ‘Submit Payment’ will charge the required amount from the patient’s credit card. ‘Print Receipt’ may be clicked to print receipt for the amount charged.

Recent patient’s prescriptions are displayed under ‘Prescriptions’ heading for easy reading. Clicking on ‘View all’ will redirect you to @Prescriptions page.

At the upper right corner is the ‘New Appointment’ button. Refer to @Booking an Appointment for its details.
