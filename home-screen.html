Home Screen

The ‘Home’ screen typically provides a central hub where users can access essential features and information including appointment schedules, prescription updates, and relevant notifications.

Currently due appointments and currently prescribed medications are shown on the screen under the Appointment and Prescription headings. If any payment is to be made for an appointment, the ‘Make Payment’ button may be clicked. An invoice with provider’s name, patient’s visit details and amount to be charged are displayed. Patient is required to fill in his or her credit card details and billing address particulars. Clicking on ‘Submit Payment’ will charge the required amount from the patient’s credit card. ‘Print Receipt’ may be clicked to print receipt for the amount charged.
