Prescriptions

Prescriptions are necessary for accurately documenting and managing medication orders. If the Prescription page is selected, it will display all medications a particular patient is taking or has recently taken. To the right side of each entry is a ‘Refill’ option.

‘Refill’ option is used for medication replenishment. It ensures treatment continuity and management of chronic conditions. When ‘Refill’ is selected, a ‘Refill Request’ is displayed. Completing the form and selecting ‘Refill Request’ will send a refill demand to the provider, on whose approval medication restock will occur.
