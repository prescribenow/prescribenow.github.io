Prescriptions

Prescriptions are necessary for accurately documenting and managing medication orders. Below ‘Appointments’ is ‘Prescriptions’. If this link is selected, it will display all medications a particular patient is taking or has recently taken. To the right side of each entry is a ‘Refill’ option.

‘Refill’ option is used for medication replenishment. It ensures treatment continuity and management of chronic conditions. When ‘Refill’ is selected, a ‘Refill Request’ is displayed. Completing the form and selecting ‘Refill Request’ will send a refill demand to the provider, on whose approval medication restock will occur.
At the right side of the panel, another ‘Prescriptions’ heading is displayed. Recent patient’s prescriptions are displayed here for easy reading.

Clicking on ‘View all’ will display the same screen as is displayed when ‘Prescriptions’ is selected from the left panel.
