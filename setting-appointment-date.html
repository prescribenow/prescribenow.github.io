Setting Appointment Date

To set an ‘Appointment Date’, click on the calendar symbol at the ‘From’ side, and select a starting date.

Likewise, select the date at the ‘To’ side.

Click on a provider’s name from the list given.

Select the 'Appointment Status' of your preference. There are five available statuses to choose from:
i. Pending - an appointment that has reached its scheduled start time but has not commenced yet.
ii. Canceled - indicates that a scheduled appointment has been intentionally terminated.
iii. Started – an appointment currently in progress.
iv. Scheduled – an appointment that has been arranged for a specific date and time in the future.
v. Completed - scheduled appointment that took place on time, and that now has been concluded.

In the end, click on ‘Filter’. You may download the Huddle Sheet by selecting the download symbol under the ‘Filter’ button. The downloaded file will be saved with a .CSV extension and can be opened with MS Excel.

To select an imaging order, the provider can mark the corresponding checkbox for each imaging procedure required for the patient. Click on ‘Add’ to append the imaging orders to the patient’s name. Additional imaging orders can be included by searching for suitable ones. To remove selected imaging orders, click on the 'bin' symbol next to each entry.

Additional imaging orders not listed here can be added in the ‘Additional Tests’ section.

At the end, a ‘Summary’ section provides a concise overview of the imaging procedures to be conducted on a patient. The summary can be printed or faxed, depending on the provider’s requirement. Please click on the checkbox to enable the ‘Submit’ button.

When the imaging order has been submitted, two things happen. Firstly, the patient has a new imaging order entry against his or her name.

Secondly, a PDF file is automatically downloaded into the system titled ‘Imaging Order Request’.

The downloaded file states patient’s particulars and all imaging orders to be conducted on the patient.
