On Selecting a Patient's Name

When you click on any patient’s name, a detailed description of the patient opens in front of you.
The ‘Patients’ page can be accessed from the home screen. When At the upper right corner, a drop-down menu titled ‘New’ displays a list of options including Appointment, Prescription and Lab Order. Selecting the desired option enables you to organize a new instance according to your preference.

The three dots alongside ‘New’ is used to reset a patient’s website password.

The other information is arranged in tabs. The tabs, in order, are:

  • Patient Information
  • Documents
  • Messages
  • Prescriptions
  • Encounters
  • History
  • Vitals
  • Labs
  • Imaging Order
