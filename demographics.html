Demographics

Patients can be searched according to their age, gender and clinical data. Find the first two options under the ‘Demographics’ section. Clinical data is at the bottom left of the screen.
If age becomes the searching criteria, enter a number depicting the age of the patient. Use less than ‘<’, or greater than ‘>’, or equal to ‘=’ or ‘between’ to successfully search for required patient name. Select ‘Generate List’ to execute the search. The other way to search for a patient is by searching gender of patients.

A search for patients can also be conducted by searching for their Clinical profile. To do this, click on the checkbox marked ‘Active Problems in Clinical Profile’. Enter the ICD-10 score that indicates the patient’s illness. In the displayed screenshot, R33 code is used for ‘Retention of Urine’. Select ‘Generate List’ to display patient particulars with the mentioned illness.
