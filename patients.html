Patients

Patients are people who visit a healthcare facility to seek treatment and undergo examinations. Their health records, personal information, medical history, diagnoses, and treatment details are stored within the facility. This ensures delivery of quality healthcare while guaranteeing patient safety.
The ‘Patients’ page can be accessed from the home screen. When selected, it displays a list of patients with their name, gender, age, and date of birth.

A search option at the top allows you to find names easily. Typing the initial letters of a name enables you to search through all names until you locate the desired one.

An arrow at the side of the search area is clicked to include gender and age with the list of patients.
