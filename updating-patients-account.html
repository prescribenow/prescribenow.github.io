Updating Patient’s account

A patient’s account contains personal details of the patient, such as date of birth, address, email, and phone. These details are viewable but not editable. The patient may change the account’s password, enable its two-step verification, and adjust the time zone. To access a patient’s account, click on the patient’s name at the upper right corner, then select ‘My Account’.

Once the required modifications are complete, select 'Update' to confirm the changes.
