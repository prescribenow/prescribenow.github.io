Starting an Encounter

1. To start an ‘Encounter’, come to ‘Upcoming Appointments’ on the ‘Home’ screen.
2. Press ‘Start’ or ‘Resume’ against the name of ‘Patient’ for whom the Encounter is being initiated.
3. If the appointment is not ‘Follow-Up’, you will be redirected to Encounter tab view.
Refer to @Patients for details of Patient Information and Documents Tab.
