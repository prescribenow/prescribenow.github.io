Booking an Appointment

A patient can book an appointment by clicking on the ‘New Appointment’ button at the upper right section of the screen.

Appointment Type’ menu displays two default types of appointments, Consultation and Follow-up, and any additional types added by the facility.
i. Consultation: A consultation visit involves a patient meeting with a healthcare professional at a healthcare facility, to discuss and assess his or her medical condition.
ii. Follow up: A follow-up visit is a subsequent appointment that a patient attends to review and monitor his or her health progress after an initial consultation.

Virtual appointments are booked for convenience, accessibility, minimizing travel, and addressing non-emergency medical concerns. If a ‘Virtual’ appointment is booked, the ‘Facilities’ option will not be displayed. These appointments are remote in nature.
‘In-Person’ appointments involves physically visiting a healthcare provider's location for a face-to-face consultation or examination. When this type of appointment is booked, the ‘Facilities’ option is displayed.

Adding all related information and clicking on the ‘Schedule’ button will display a screen titled ‘Appointment Reserved’. Please note that an appointment is confirmed after advance payment has been made, otherwise, it will be cancelled in 15 minutes. Click on ‘Proceed to Payment’.
The following two screens will be shown:

The patient is required here to fill in credit card details and confirm his or her appointment. An option to cancel the appointment is also given.
Once a patient’s appointment has been confirmed, information is sent to the provider and reservation is confirmed.
