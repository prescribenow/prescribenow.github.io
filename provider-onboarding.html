Provider Onboarding

"Provider onboarding" is the process of bringing new healthcare providers, such as physicians, nurses, or other medical professionals, into a hospital or medical facility.

To register a provider, select the following:

Enter all relevant information, such as name, email and mobile phone. All text fields with an asterisk must be filled out as a requirement. ‘User Role’ refers to the specific role or access level that will be assigned to you. User roles are defined to ensure that individuals have appropriate access and permissions within the software.
The chosen user role determines the specific fields that are accessible to you. As an illustration, a physician needs to provide their 'NPI' number and 'Specialty,' whereas a 'Nurse Practitioner' is only expected to complete the 'Specialty' section.

A provider may select more than one facility at a time.

Ensure that your ‘User Status’ is set to Active. If the user status is marked as inactive:
• no invitation email will be sent
• the provider’s information will be stored in the system
• the provider will not have the ability to log into the system.

By now, name of the provider has been added into the system, but status of the provider is set to ‘Pending’. To change status to ‘Active’, the provider should access and reply to the sent email.

Click on the link given in the email.

You will be directed to a screen where you will add and reconfirm your website password. Click on ‘Submit’ to activate your account.

Your status will change from ‘Pending’ to ‘Active’.
