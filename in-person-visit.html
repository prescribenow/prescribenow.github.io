In-Person Visit

An in-person visit refers to a traditional, face-to-face meeting between a healthcare provider and patient. Numerous individuals contribute to the care of a patient, from the moment they arrive at a healthcare facility until the time they depart. Some of these people are physicians, physician assistants, nurses, medical assistants, and billing specialists.

An In-Person visit starts when a patient has registered an appointment with a physician. This detail is shown to a responsible person known as Staff. Staff is responsible for checking-in the patient and assigning him or her an examination room titled here as ‘Exam Room’.
