History

The History tab contains many sections.
· ‘Diagnosis History’: contains patient's past medical diagnoses.
· ‘Medication History’: includes patient's past and current use of medications.
· ‘Family Medication History’: involves details about the illnesses and medical conditions that are common within the family.
· ‘Surgical History’: contains person's past surgical procedures or operations.
· Hospitalizations: includes past admissions to hospitals or inpatient medical facilities.
· Social History: contains patients’ social and environmental factors that may impact their health and well-being.
