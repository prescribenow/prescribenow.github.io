Documents Tab

Documents such as Lab Reports, Patient Consent Forms and Discharge Summaries can be uploaded through this tab. After entering the document name, select the type of document by checking one of the boxes from the six given types.

After uploading a document, check the box titled ‘Create task for document review’ if you want the document to be reviewed by any other physician. A document review task will be automatically generated, and the healthcare provider will be able to view the uploaded document.
