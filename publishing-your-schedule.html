Publishing your Schedule

1. Click on ‘Appointments’ from the left pane of the screen.

2. If you wish to publish an upcoming schedule, click on ‘Schedule’, and then on ‘Publish.

3. Select Provider whose schedule is to be published.

4. Select Facility to which this schedule will be associated.

5. Start date is always pre-selected, i.e., today’s date. If you want to change the date, click the calendar icon to select your desired dates. When a ‘Start Date’ has been selected, the ‘End Date’ will automatically be added a week later. The weekdays from Monday to Sunday will correspond to the dates added.

6. Enter your arrival and departure time for each day. If you work in the morning and early afternoon from Monday to Thursday, specify your hours in the provided fields. Your screen should look like this:

7. You can add multiple times for a day. Click on ‘Add Row’ if your available time is not continuous.

8. Pressing the ‘Save’ button will publish your schedule.

9. You can look up your published schedule by clicking on ‘Appointments’ from the left pane of the Home page. Next, select Schedule from the upper right corner of your screen, and then click on View. Further, you can select provider, facility and dates between which you want to view schedule.
