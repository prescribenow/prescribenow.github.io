Medication Orders

1. The Medication page displays a text field wherein generic, or brand names of medicines may be entered. The example below shows various options for Amoxicillin.

2. While dispensing Amoxicillin 500mg capsules, you will be asked to enter its ‘Sig Information’.

Multiple choices are available for selection. To explore additional preferences, you can scroll downward. Options vary; you can select number of capsules / tablets to be taken per day, you can designate number of days certain medication has to be taken, etc. To do the latter, simply input number of days in the 'Days' Supply' text field, and the quantity of capsules/tablets to be provided will be adjusted automatically. The concept of 'Refills' is in place to prevent patients from depleting their medication supply. Generally, long-term treatments necessitate the need for refills.
If the patient requires medication in a form not listed in the Sig information, you may select ‘Enter sig manually’ and type in the necessary information. You can also add further notes or instructions for the pharmacist. When done, select ‘Add to Prescription’.
A quick overview of chosen medication, dispensing method, and status appears below. You can also edit or delete your prescription. Pressing ‘Continue’ will take you to the next section.

3. Once medications for a patient have been selected, select the Pharmacy that will provide them. Enquire if the patient has any pharmacy in mind. Select it and set its proximity from the patient’s residence. Proximity options vary from 5 miles to 100 miles. If the patient agrees to receive medications from the said pharmacy in the future as well, set as ‘Patient Preferred’. For all future prescriptions, this pharmacy will be automatically selected.

4. Order Summary serves as a reference for both the patient and the physician. At the end of the summary are three ‘Order Type’ options: e-Prescribe, Fax and Print. Be sure to check the box before selecting any of these options.

You can also view the medication orders made on the ‘Patients’ tab by selecting ‘Prescriptions’.

Medication can also be accessed in the following ways:

  • Encounters tab view > Assessment and Plan > Add Medication Order (bottom right corner)

  • SOAP Note > Scroll down to Plan section > Add Medication

  • Patients > Specific Patient’s name > New (top right corner) > Provider Note > Add Medication
