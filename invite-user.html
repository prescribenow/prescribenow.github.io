Invite User

Invite User refers to a functionality that allows focal person to send invitations to individuals, granting them access to specific practice. A physician working in one tenant may wish to simultaneously give time to another tenant. ‘Invite User’ is an option that can make this possible.

To access this feature, click on ‘Admin’ on the Home screen. Select ‘Practice Users’ and click on ‘Invite User’ at the upper right corner of the screen.

Search the name of the individual. Click on the name when it appears alongside its NPI, and then select ‘Invite’. An email will be sent to the concerned person’s email address.

The email addresses the invitee and mentions the name of the inviter. It also gives details about the facility being invited to and the rewards this invitation carries.

When the invitee clicks on the given link, he or she is accepted to the facility’s portal and the newly added facility name appears under the present facility.
