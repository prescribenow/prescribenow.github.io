Adding a new Facility

To add a new facility, select ‘Add Facility’ from the top right corner. Numerous text fields and dropdown menus become visible, offering a variety of options for selection. See below for a sample:

Place of Service refers to the location or department (within a medical care facility) where a particular medical service or procedure is being provided. There are twenty-three options to choose from, ranging from ‘Pharmacy’ to ‘Emergency Room – Hospital’.

‘Number of Rooms’ carries a ‘Room Prefix’ that you add at the beginning to uniquely identify and categorize different types of rooms within the healthcare facility. This can include inpatient hospital, urgent care facility, emergency room and pharmacy. The prefix is added to the number of rooms you have chosen for your facility.

Setting up your facility as a ‘Primary Facility’ determines that it is going to be staffed by primary care providers such as general practitioners, family physicians, nurse practitioners, and physician assistants. When you check the provided checkbox, the said facility appears with a little ‘Primary’ mark after its name.
