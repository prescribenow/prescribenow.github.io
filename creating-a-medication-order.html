Prescription – A Medication Order

Prescription is equally important for a patient and healthcare provider. It helps providers to prescribe and administer medicine that is in correct dosage and free from drug interactions.

Creating a Medication Order

1. To create a Medication Order, click on ‘Patients’ from the home screen.

2. Select any specific patient’s name from the ones listed.

3. Click on ‘New’ at the top right corner.

4. Under ‘New’, select ‘Prescription’.
