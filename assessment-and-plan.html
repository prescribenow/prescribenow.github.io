Assessment & Plan

Assessment is the physician’s written evaluation of a patient's condition. Plan is the proposed course of action for the patient's treatment and care.

A physician can directly add diagnosis in the Assessment & Plan tab. Select ‘Add Diagnosis’ for this and type in the details.

The "Plan" details the physician’s recommended steps for treating and taking care of the patient. Medication and Lab Orders can be added directly from here as well.

Towards the end, you will come across three options, namely:
• Save
• Summary
• End Consultation

The ‘Save’ feature is utilized to store all entered data associated with the current encounter. Do note that switching tabs also automatically saves your data.

‘Summary’, when selected, sums up all information onto a window titled ‘Patient Encounter Note’. This is a summary of all the patient data that has been entered in the Encounter section. It provides editing facilities before it can be signed or exported as PDF. It should be kept in mind that without a diagnosis, a physician will not be able to sign it. The physician may ‘Resume’ to enter patient data until the Patient Encounter Note is completed. Once signed, it can only be viewed, but not edited.

The ‘End ‘Consultation’ option finalizes the current patient consultation session, closing the associated records and concluding the interaction.

Three different icons appear at the end of a signed encounter. The leftmost displays the encounter details. The middle icon is labelled ‘Show Addendums’. Rightmost icon is used to create a ‘Superbill’. An addendum is a document that provides additional information regarding a patient’s medical treatment. Superbill is a detailed charges list of medical diagnosis or services provided to a patient.

There are many other ways to initiate an Encounter:

  • Encounters > Specific Encounter Resume > (If Encounter is a new / custom) > Encounters (tab view)

  • Home > Pending Encounters (on top) > Select Specific Encounters > (If type is a new / custom) > Encounters (tab view)

  • Patients > Specific Patient’s name > Patient Information > Pending Encounters (on top) > Resume(If type is a new / custom) > Encounters (tab view)
