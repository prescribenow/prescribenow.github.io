Calendar

Calendar serves many purposes, some of which are appointment scheduling, resource management and coordination between medical professionals and their patients.

To access Calendar, click on Appointments from the home screen.

The Calendar will open in front of you, marking todayâ€™s date with a pink circle. Dates are presented to you in a month-wise format by default. Week-wise and day-wise presentation of dates is possible by clicking on the appropriate choice at the upper right corner of the screen.

Please see below for week-wise and day-wise screenshots.

Clicking on any date will list down all appointments scheduled for the said date on the right side of the screen.

Clicking on the three vertically aligned dots allows you to make changes to the appointment. You may reschedule the appointment for another date or cancel it altogether. If a meeting has already been initiated or its starting time has passed, the meeting cannot be rescheduled or cancelled.
