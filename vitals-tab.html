Vitals Tab

The ‘Vitals’ tab contains the first indicators of a patient's medical condition. It consists of temperature, blood pressure, pulse, oxygen saturation level, height, weight and BMI. It must be kept in mind that Vital readings have a limited human range. For example, ‘Temp’ in Fahrenheit has a range of 90 to 105. Any attempt to enter values lesser or greater than prescribed returns a message. An example is given below:

If a temperature value of 106°F is to be entered, click on ‘Confirm’ to add the vital. Likewise, abnormal values of ‘Blood Pressure’ and ‘Pulse’ can only be entered if the physician confirms its presence.

The ‘Vitals’ tab also contains the patient’s history (if any). This further helps the health provider to better assess and monitor the patient’s medical condition. Saving the patient’s data helps to store the most recent edits and updates.
